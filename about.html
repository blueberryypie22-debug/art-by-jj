Art by JJ was made to hold my recent and upcoming art projects. I always wanted a place for my work that wasn't social media and that could give me the freedom to create a complete visual image of who I am and my style.
I wouldn't title myself as an artist but I have been making art since I was 14. At the beginning I just wanted to learn the skill so I stuck to the basics, making mostly portraits and sometimes still pieces. Currrently, art is much more personal to me. I'm interested in perspective art and I'm inspired by the UK's green spaces, churches and country side as well as Ethel Cain.
Expect to see experimental pieces and the rest of my 'House' series.
